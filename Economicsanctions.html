To comply with U.S. and international counter-terrorism laws, WCS must screen each subrecipient, along with its senior management (Chief Executive Officer/President, Chief Operating Officer, and Chief Financial Officer, or their equivalents) and key Officers of the Board of Directors/Trustees, if applicable (Chair, Vice-Chair, Secretary, Treasurer).

To proceed with a subaward, please provide for each relevant individual:

  • Full name
  • Title within the organization
  • Country of citizenship

WCS uses the Lexis/Nexis Bridger Insight platform for these screenings. By submitting this information, your organization confirms the individuals listed have consented to this process.
